Andrea Kennedy, Web Developer & Code Instructor

Web Developer

I am a Ruby on Rails and Javascript web developer with project management experience. In June 2013, I retrained from historian to web developer by taking the 3-month immersive course at General Assembly. Since then I have worked for a small digital agency and now work as a freelance web developer.

Instructor

I work as a contracted instructor for General Assembly on their Front End Web Development (FEWD) course for adults. I am also a Teaching Assistant for the Programming for Non-Programmers and Wordpress Bootcamp workshops, and have spoken at numerous information sessions and events.

Writer

With an educational background in English and History, I have extensive experience writing both academically and for the web. I kept a blog about becoming a web developer, have written travel, digital and academic articles, and conducted extensive archival and secondary research and reports for English Heritage.

Work With Me

Throughout the development process, I use the agile approach, constantly iterating through each stage of client liaison, project planning, prototyping, designing, developing, and testing. This keeps you informed and encourages constant corrections to make sure I'm headed down the right track to producing the most accurate and highest quality product.

I use a mixture of technologies and select the best ones to suit each project. My favourite go-tos are Ruby on Rails, Javascript and jQuery, HTML5/CSS, mobile-first development, and ensuring cross-browser compatibility and responsive design.

Finally, I can perform the following tasks for every project:

  • Fully outlined technical specification
  • Sitemap and full features list
  • Project timeline and budget estimates
  • Sprint planning
  • Prototyping
  • Front-end Development
  • Back-end Development
  • QA Testing
  • Deployment and Hosting
  • Training with CMS (if required)

If working with a designer I can also liaise with them to provide:

  • User experience and journey planning
  • Wireframing
  • Full site design
